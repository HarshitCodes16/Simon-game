1) First press any key on keyboard to start with.
2) Then you would notice computer to flash one of the buttons.
3) Your role is to hit that button.
4) Now computer again clicks another button.
5) Now you have to tap the previous button and the button which computer pressed in 2nd step.
   (Basically it's a memory recall game.)
6) Once you hit the wrong pattern , the game is over.
